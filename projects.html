Ballot Check - Wisconsin

Fall 2020

I'm currently designing and developing a website to educate Wisconsin voters about the representatives they're voting for in their district's ballot. This website, Ballot Check, provides the steps necessary to vote and educates voters on the election's key issues, like COVID-19, the economy, healthcare, criminal justice, reproductive rights, and more. In just a team of myself and one other engineer, we are using the Cicero API within WIX to pull the correct polling and district information and display to users. Link to website coming soon!

Music Cognition Research

Fall 2017

Last Fall, I researched with Music Psychologist Aniruddh Patel if a musical synchronous activity between two people leads to an increase in intentional binding. I studied background research on joint music making, group cohesion, sense of agency, and self-other blurring. Ultimately, I coded three separate experiments in Javascript and HTML/CSS to track intentional binding on EvoThings using Android development tools.
