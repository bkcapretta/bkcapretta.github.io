Events App

In my Human Computer Interaction class in Spring 2018, I worked in a group all semester to design an app of our creation. We liked how people can see, create, and attend events on Facebook, but wanted to streamline this feature in one app. With this idea in mind, we created the app, "Events". In creating the designs, I learned how to use Sketch and had way more fun than I expected designing each page. My contributions are displayed below.
