Music

Singing

I had the pleasure of singing as an Alto in Shir Appeal, Tufts' Only Mixed-Gender Jewish A Cappella group, in my four years at Tufts University. As a Sophomore and Senior, I led as Music Director, which involved leading rehearsal seven hours per week, running the audition/callback process each semester, arranging music for the group, and producing our albums.

Our most recent album Common Threads recently came out this past Fall! A big part of my involvement in the group revolved around arranging songs (including Romemu, Meah Chuz Or, Like the Dawn, and Yo M'enamori on the new album). If you want to hear my solo on the album, check out Like the Dawn. I've had the exciting opportunity to join the album team in producing the last two albums. The last one was Perspective which was nominated for three Contemporary A Cappella music awards.

All of the music we sing has some kind of Jewish significance; we sing in many languages including English, Ladino, Yemenite Arabic, Portuguese, Russian, Yiddish, and now Amharic. I love spending time every week making music with this lovely group of people. As a music nerd, I must say that making harmony is simply one of the more beautiful things in life.

Some solos and duets: Like the Dawn, Ana El Na, Ivdu Et Hashem B'Simcha

Arrangements for the group: Yo M'enamori, Romemu, Meah Chuz Or

Jazz Combo

For three years, I have been a vocalist in one of Tufts' Jazz Improv Combos. With an amazing professor Nando Michelin, I've learned a lot about making melodies my own, creating new arrangements, and not being afraid to try something new.

Check out the recordings that came from my most recent concert! Songs include Life on Mars by David Bowie, Nakamarra by Hiatus Kaiyote, and Time's Lie by Chick Corea to name a few.

Piano

I have been classically trained on the piano since I was five years-old. Some of my favorite pieces to play include:

  • Arabesque No. 1 by Debussy
  • Beethoven's Piano Sonata No.12 in A-flat major
  • Milonga del Angel by Astor Piazzolla
  • Six Morceaux Op. 11 - Waltz by Rachmaninoff
  • Arabesque by Schumann(and really anything by Schumann)
  • Rondo alla Turca by Mozart

Radio and Recording

WMFO 91.5 Medford Freeform Radio Station

For four semesters, my friend Jacob and I hosted a fun radio show called "Over Easy". We love music and talking about brunch because it is objectively the best meal. Check us out at 91.5 WMFO or see more on our Facebook page.

The semester everyone decided to study abroad I became more involved with Studio B and Studio D where you can set-up bands or musicians with microphones and amps. I learned how to use the sound board so their music can be played live on a show, or recorded into what could be their future EP!
