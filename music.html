Shir Appeal

I joined Shir Appeal (Tufts' Only Coed Jewish A Cappella group) last year and am now the Music Director of the group. All the music we sing has some kind of Jewish significance; we don't just sing in Hebrew, but also in English, Ladino, Yemenite Arabic, Portuguese, Russian, and Yiddish. I love spending time making music with the people in this group. As a music nerd, I must say making harmony is simply one of the most beautiful things in life.

As Music Director, I lead every rehearsal (which is seven hours per week) with warmups and song-learning. I run the audition and callback process twice a year. After organizing the song selection process, I arrange music for the group and mangage the recording process. I just produced our most recent album "Perspective" with Plaid Productions. You can now hear us on Spotify!

Check out the song I arranged this past semester in Ladino called "Yo M'enamori" originally performed by Casata Deleon. And I also got to sing a fun duet about healing with my friend Sarah in "Ana El Na".

Piano

I have been classically trained on the piano since I was five years-old. Some of my favorite pieces to play include:

  • Arabesque No. 1 by Debussy
  • Beethoven's Piano Sonata No.12 in A-flat major
  • Milonga del Angel by Astor Piazzolla

Radio

Every Sunday from 10-11AM, my friend Jacob and I have fun playing music and chatting on our radio show called "Over Easy". Check us out at 91.5 WMFO or like us on Facebook.
